Background

Anxiety Disorder are characterized by feelings of worry,anxiety,or fear that are strong enough to interfere with daily activities. Everyone almost certainly has experience what is called anxiety,anxiety,worry,and panic. In everyday life,anxiety is a natural thing that happens to every individual as a person's reaction when experiencing stress is often accompanied by an anxiety. However, if an individual can't control or reduce the anxiety in a situation where people are generally able to handle anxiety without the difficulties that are considered so meaningful then in this case has been said to be a deviation.
